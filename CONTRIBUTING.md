# Contributing to Uble OS

Thank you for your interest in contributing to Uble OS!

Uble OS is an open-source project created and maintained by **Jeb K.** Contributions are welcome and appreciated.

## Before You Contribute

By submitting code, documentation, artwork, bug fixes, features, or any other contribution to Uble OS, you agree to the terms below.

## How to Contribute

1. Fork the repository.
2. Create a new branch for your changes.
3. Make your improvements.
4. Test your changes when possible.
5. Submit a Pull Request with a description of what you changed.

## Attribution

All contributors should acknowledge that their work is based on Uble OS.

If you publish a modified version, please include:

> Based on Uble OS by Jeb K.

Please also consider using:

* #UbleOS
* #UbleOSDev

when sharing your work online.

## Contributor Agreement

By submitting a contribution to Uble OS, you confirm that:

* You created the contribution yourself, or have the legal right to submit it.
* Your contribution does not knowingly violate the rights of others.
* You retain ownership of your original contributions.
* You grant Jeb K. and the Uble OS project a perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free license to use, modify, distribute, and include your contribution in Uble OS.

This allows Uble OS to continue growing while ensuring contributors receive credit for their work.

## Pull Request Guidelines

Please:

* Keep changes focused and organized.
* Explain what your changes do.
* Be respectful and constructive.
* Follow existing project structure and coding style when possible.

## Forks and Derivative Projects

Forks and modified versions are allowed under the project's license.

Please:

* Credit Uble OS and Jeb K.
* Clearly state what changes were made.
* Avoid claiming that your version is the official Uble OS project unless it has been approved by the maintainer.

## Maintainer Rights

The maintainer of Uble OS (Jeb K.) reserves the right to:

* Accept or reject pull requests.
* Request changes before merging.
* Remove contributions that violate project rules or licensing requirements.
* Guide the future direction of the project.

## Thank You

Every bug report, feature suggestion, code contribution, and documentation update helps improve Uble OS for everyone.
